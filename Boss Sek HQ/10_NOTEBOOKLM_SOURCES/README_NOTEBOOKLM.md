# NotebookLM Source Rooms

Use these folders to collect files before uploading to NotebookLM.

Start with only these four rooms:

1. `AI News & Tools Room`
2. `Business & Marketing Strategy Room`
3. `SK Suki Research Room`
4. `9szek Artist Room`

## Rule

NotebookLM is not permanent memory.

After extracting insights, save the final summary back to Notion.

## What To Put In NotebookLM

- Long PDFs
- Reports
- SOP manuals
- Competitor documents
- Old campaign files
- Product specs
- Research packs

## What Not To Put

- Random files without a research question
- Final knowledge that should live in Notion
- Every business mixed into one notebook
